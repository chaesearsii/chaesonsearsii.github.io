WALL-E

Kinematic System Modeled on Wall-E

This personal project represents my first fully independent design and build effort outside of school. The teleoperated quadruped robot was entirely designed, 3D printed, assembled, and programmed by me.

The system demonstrates mechanical design, fabrication, and robotics integration skills while serving as a platform for future research and development during my master's studies in Robotics and Autonomous Systems.

Technologies Used

  • 3D Printing
  • Embedded Programming
  • Mechanical Design
  • Robotics Control
